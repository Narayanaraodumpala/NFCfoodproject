Which work-from-home personality are you ?
      
The times are such that most of us now have to work from home. While some of us find comfort in not having to go to work, others find it difficult to make it through the day. But you gotta do what you gotta do. If you too are working from home and miss having someone around, we’ve got your favourite company: food!
Read on to see which work-from-home personality matches your style and which dish goes best with your vibe!
1. The OG procrastinator

             
They don’t set an alarm and wakes up on their own time. They do things at their own pace and try to avoid connecting with their peers for work. They need a break every half an hour and their lunch time
turns into one-more-episode-before-I-start cycle. A thali will be the perfect meal, so they can savour it at their own speed.
2 . The message flooder
      
These are the proactive ones who are always on their toes, sorry fingertips. Your work notifications don’t stop ringing, thanks to them. They are the first one to read all messages on the office group and their reply game is strong AF. Although they’ve got all the energy they need, some coffee will keep them powering through the day!

These are the ones who don’t just work efficiently but also manage to clean their house, get supplies from the market, walk their dogs and even find the time to watch their favourite shows while they’re at it.
