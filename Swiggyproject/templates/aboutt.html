Our Values



                          Resilience

                          Acceptance                                                                      

  • Feedback is never taken personally, we break it into positive pieces and strive to work on each and every element even more effectively.
                          Ownership                                                                      

  • Feedback is never taken personally, we break it into positive pieces and strive to work on each and every element even more effectively.


                          Humility                                                        

  • It’s always ‘us’ over ‘me’. We don’t lose ourselves in pride or confidence during individual successes, but focus on being our simple selves in every which way.
                          Spark

            Our Journey       

  • Back in 20019, all it took was an idea to enable
    digital access to thousands of restaurant menus.
    Three passionate foodies who hated waiting in lines,
    drove around Delhi to collect menus from restaurants,
    scan them and put them online. Their idea has now
    grown into the vision that drives our team of
    5000+ people everyday.
